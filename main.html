What is the highest Bio-safety level that your workers are trained for?

Biosafety levels correspond to different levels of safety. BSL 1 labs require minimal training, as none of the bacteria worked with are harmful. BSL 2 labs work with midly dangerous bacteria, and BSL 3 and 4 labs are worked in by extensively trained professionals.

How do you check for unsealed containers?

If products are not sealed, contaminants may be able to enter them. Tests range from simple, visual tests, to vaccum leak testing, in which changes in pressure correspond to leaks when placed in a vacuum, and microbial ingress testing, in which packages are submerged in a solution of bacteria to see if they can get in.
